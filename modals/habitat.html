FKNMS Sub-regions

In many analyses it makes sense to split the Florida Reef Tract into sub-regions [T. J. T. MurdochR. B. Aronson 1999]. The regional subdivision of the reef tract is not well standardized, however. The definition of the subregions used here and an explanation of this choice is discussed on the fk-subdiv page.
